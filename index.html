Our Story

Nakuru's Hidden Gem

Karibu Nakuru, karibu familia! We are a small family-owned restaurant right in the heart of Nakuru — a place that has grown into a gathering spot where locals and visitors come together to enjoy authentic Kenyan meals.

Everything is made with love and fresh ingredients from our own backyard and trusted local farmers. No fancy stuff. Just honest, delicious food that reminds you why Kenyan cuisine is world-class.

"Hakuna matata when the food is this good."

We cook slow so you can enjoy every bite. Eat up and leave with a full belly and happy heart.
